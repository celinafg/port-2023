About me

Hi! I’m Celina Garcia, a Seattle-based UX designer from the Philippines. I graduated with a degree in Marketing Management, worked in the industry until I bit the bullet and transitioned from marketing to software engineering (which was extremely challenging).

Aside from the technical differences in process of both marketing and software roles, some things stayed the same. Being able to work in a team, communicate well, advocate and be persistent are skills that I brought to both roles. The perspective I’ve gained from both roles and experiences have led me to user centered design, with its interdisciplinary nature.

Ultimately, I want to create or work on products that meaningfully impact users lives. Growing up in the Philippines where there’s a large gap in what people need and what is available, I’ve seen firsthand how technology can transform lives (whether through making something more accessible or inclusive) for the better.

When I'm not building things or thinking about building things, you can usually find me hobbying it out with yoga, keyboards, SCUBA diving, or trying out different hydrations for sourdough!
